ChildSafe

online emergency plans



What's so special about it?

User Friendly

Easy to use and easy to understand. Intuitive and simple. Appealing. No need to worry about learning a new program, or using cumbersome technology. ChildSafe will lead you step by step to your destination.

Fully Responsive

The optimal viewing and interaction experience. Easy reading and navigation across a wide range of devices. So keep on using your desktops, laptops, tablets and mobile phones to log in, and accomplish your job the easy way.

Secure

Your data is secure. That's why we partnered with Caspio for data encryption and secure cloud storage. We control the data, and we restrict access to records based on the law and our business needs.

Why should I care?

Child Care Providers

Gone are the days when you had to fill out the paperwork and send it to our agency. Now, you can easily comply with the law and submit emergency plans on the fly. You can edit emergency plans whenever you need to. And you can make changes from any type of device you currently use.

Emergency Management

Gone are the days when you had to sift through your archive to find evacuation routes and relocation sites for daycare facilities. Now you and first responders can use this valuable information during emergencies. Valuable information that can make a difference between life and death.

State Administrators

Gone are the days when you had to go through our archive to see if daycare operators comply with the law. Now, you can easily search through available records, based on any criteria you choose. It's that simple.

Parents

Gone are the days when daycare operators couldn’t update emergency plans easily. Now, it’s also much easier for them to provide you with updated emergency records on time. And parents, you are entitled to it. It’s the law.
